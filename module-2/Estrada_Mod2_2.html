An Introduction to Andrew Estrada

My name is Andrew Estrada. I was born and raised in the Illinois Tri-city area. My family came here from Mexico in 1984. I'm the youngest of 3 children.

Some of my favorite things are skateboarding, playing instruments, board games, and video games. Above all, video games are my favorite thing. I like watching, playing and creating video games.

Competition level fighting video games, like Street Fighter and Mortal Kombat, are my favorite. I go to events to help organize, maintain, and of course, participate. Every year I go to at least two major national events. Those are Frosty Faustings and Combo Breaker. Both take place in Illinois.

I'm taking these classes to get closer to my goal as a video game developer, while also keeping a realistic outlook and getting a more broad degree that I can use elsewhere. I hope to someday make a video game that matters to people, the same way there are video games that matter to me. Competitive games have given me a sense of community, while story-rich games have given me narratives that have shaped my life.
